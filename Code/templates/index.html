About Us

At PHQ - 9, we are dedicated to providing a user-friendly and accessible way for individuals to track their medical information. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health and wellness journey by simplifying the process of storing and retrieving important medical data.

Our team is made up of experienced healthcare professionals and web developers who understand the importance of accurate and secure medical record keeping. We are committed to providing a warm and fuzzy user experience that puts individuals at ease and helps them feel confident in managing their health.

Whether you're a patient looking for a convenient way to keep track of your medical history, or a healthcare provider seeking an efficient way to collect and store patient data, PHQ - 9 is here to help. We are constantly striving to improve our platform and provide the best possible experience for our users.
